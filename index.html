About Us

I founded my bracelet and ring business in early 2024, and it's been an exciting journey ever since. Being 13, I poured my creativity and passion into designing unique and stylish accessories that people love. I ship all over Dubai, ensuring that everyone can enjoy my beautiful bracelets and rings. It's amazing to see my designs being worn by so many, and I'm grateful for the support and enthusiasm from our customers!

My collection is curated to suit every occasion, from daily wear to special events. Whether you’re looking for a statement piece that stands out or a delicate accessory that complements your outfit, our range offers something for every style and preference. I am dedicated to delivering exceptional customer service and ensuring that each piece brings joy and confidence to its wearer. Our mission is to celebrate individuality and style, making every moment special with our exquisite creations.

Thank you for being a part of our journey. It’s incredibly rewarding to see our designs being worn and cherished by people from all walks of life.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What materials are used in your bracelets?

▼

Our bracelets are crafted using high-quality string and high quality of acrylic colorfast beads. We ensure that every piece is durable and stylish.

How can I place an order?

▼

You can place an order through our website by selecting the desired bracelet and following the checkout process. We also accept custom orders; please contact us for more details.

Do you offer international shipping?

▼

Currently, we offer shipping all over Dubai. We are looking into expanding our shipping options in the near future.

What is your return policy?

▼

We offer a 14-day return policy on unused and undamaged items. Please ensure that the item is returned in its original packaging. For more details, refer to our return policy page.

How do I contact customer support?

▼

You can reach out to our customer support team via email at info@braceletbusiness.com or through our contact form available on the Contact Us page.

How should I care for my jewelry?

▼

To keep your jewelry looking its best, clean it regularly with a soft cloth and avoid exposure to harsh chemicals or prolonged contact with water. Store your jewelry in a cool, dry place, and consider using anti-tarnish pouches or cloths for added protection.
